Shunya offers four main services: Company Registration (Private Limited, LLP, OPC), 15CA/15CB filings for foreign remittances, Startup India DPIIT recognition, and Company Closure (STK-2 strike-off). All services are fully CA-assisted and handled online.

Company registration typically takes 7–10 working days, depending on government processing times. This includes name approval by ROC, DIN and DSC issuance for directors, drafting of MOA & AOA, and final incorporation certificate. We offer a full refund if we miss our committed timeline.

Form 15CA is a self-declaration required when remitting money outside India, and Form 15CB is a CA certificate certifying the nature of payment for tax purposes. Together they are required under Section 195 of the Income Tax Act and FEMA regulations for most foreign payments above ₹5 lakh. We file both and can typically complete same-day turnaround.
