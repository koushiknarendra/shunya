who needs to register for GST?

mandatory for businesses crossing the turnover threshold — or selling on e-commerce platforms.

₹

Turnover Threshold

mandatory above limits

If your annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakh (goods) or ₹20 lakh (services), GST registration is mandatory. For special category states, the limit is ₹10 lakh.

🛒

E-Commerce Sellers

regardless of turnover

If you sell on Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, or any other e-commerce platform, GST registration is mandatory from day one — irrespective of your turnover.

🌍

Interstate Suppliers

crossing state borders

Any business making inter-state supply of goods or services must register for GST regardless of turnover. This includes all importers, exporters, and service providers with pan-India clients.

common questions

Who needs GST registration?

Any business with annual turnover exceeding ₹40 lakh (goods) or ₹20 lakh (services) must register. Registration is also mandatory for e-commerce sellers, interstate suppliers, and businesses under the reverse charge mechanism — regardless of turnover.

What documents are needed?

PAN card, Aadhaar card, proof of business address (electricity bill or rent agreement), bank account details, and a passport-size photograph. For companies: Certificate of Incorporation and MoA/AoA. Our CA will send you the exact checklist after you sign up.

How long does it take?

Once your documents are in order, we file the application within 24 hours. The government typically issues the GSTIN within 5–7 working days. If there is an officer query, we handle it on your behalf at no extra charge.

What does Shunya charge for GST registration?

₹1,499 one-time. The government does not charge any fee for GST registration. Our fee covers document review, application preparation, portal filing, and query handling until your GSTIN is issued.

What if I delay registration?

Operating without GST registration when it is mandatory attracts a penalty of ₹10,000 or the amount of tax due — whichever is higher. Register before your first taxable invoice to stay fully compliant.
