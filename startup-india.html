why most applications get rejected

DPIIT looks for innovation, scalability, and proper documentation — most founders miss the mark

Generic innovation descriptions

DPIIT reviewers see thousands of copy-paste answers. Vague or generic descriptions lead to immediate rejection.

Failing to explain scalability

DPIIT expects your startup to demonstrate how the product or service can grow — missing this is a common mistake.

Misaligned documentation

Incomplete or incorrect documents cause delays and rejections that could have been easily avoided.
