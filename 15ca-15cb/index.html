What Are These Forms?

Two documents. One compliance requirement.

Before your bank processes a foreign payment, it needs both of these.

Form 15CA

Your declaration to the Income Tax department

Filed by you (the remitter) on the Income Tax e-filing portal before the transfer. Under Section 195(6) of the Income Tax Act, this is mandatory for most foreign payments above Rs 5 lakhs. Shunya can file it on your behalf.

Form 15CB

Your CA's independent compliance certificate

Issued by a practicing Chartered Accountant after reviewing the transaction, verifying TDS applicability, and checking the relevant DTAA. The CA uploads it with a UDIN on the portal. Shunya's CA team prepares and signs this for your remittance.
